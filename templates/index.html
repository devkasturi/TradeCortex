📈
ML Price Prediction
Linear Regression trained on 3 months of historical data. Predicts the closing price 5 trading days ahead. Model accuracy shown as R² score × 100.
⚡
EVS — Emotional Volatility Score
Our USP. Combines price volatility with sentiment-price mismatch. High EVS = price and news sentiment disagree. Use caution when EVS > 66.
💬
Sentiment Analysis
Uses TextBlob NLP to score news headlines -1.0 (negative) to +1.0 (positive). Scores above 0.1 = Positive, below -0.1 = Negative.
🎯
Recommendation Engine
Combines ML trend, sentiment, and EVS risk using a weighted score system. Thresholds adjust based on your investor mode (Conservative/Moderate/Aggressive).
🛡️
Investor Modes
Conservative requires score ≥ 4 to Buy. Moderate requires ≥ 3. Aggressive only needs ≥ 1. Set your mode in the top-right dropdown.
🗂️
Analysis History
Every analysis is saved to MySQL. View your last 20 reports in the History tab, including sentiment, EVS, and recommendation for each ticker.
